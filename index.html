WIUS Boundary Resource Satisfaction Monitoring Tool Prototype

Our purpose is to support Industrial IoT (IIoT) platform companies to maximize the satisfaction of their ecosystem participatns due to the quality optimization of the platform boundary resources offered.

What We Do

The purpose of this prototype is to demonstrate a product management support system. The support system is based on the qualitative feedback on the quality of the bpundary resources by its users. The feedback is saved in a database and displayed in a dashboard to increase the transparency of the current satisfaction state on the provided Boundary Resources. The dashboard helps the employees of a platform company, which utilizes the Boundary Resources Design Model, to prioritize the quality improvement actions in order to maximize the complementor satisfaction.

Look at the bottom for the relevant areas to submit feedback on the experience of your personal use of boundary resources or look at the sentiment visulazing dashboard regarding the offered boundary resources
